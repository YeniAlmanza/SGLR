Our Story

I started Shar'on Garden Landscaping and Renovations in 2004 with one goal in mind, to support my family. What began as a small landscaping business quickly grew into something much more. A year later, I decided to expand. With previous experience in construction, I began studying remodeling and took on hands-on projects to build my skills and confidence.

My first big remodeling project turned out great, and that success gave me the push I needed to officially start offering remodeling services about four years later. Since then, through hard work, learning, and a lot of faith, the business has continued to grow. Today, we have over 20 years of experience in landscaping and more than 16 years in remodeling.

As a fully family-owned business, we've done everything from simple yard maintenance to full home additions, bathroom makeovers, and electrical work. Through it all, I've always trusted in God to guide my path. We've come a long way, and we're passionate about continuing to provide the highest quality service to every customer we meet.
