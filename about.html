Our Story

Shar'on Garden Landscaping and Renovation was founded in 2004 with a simple goal in mind: to support a growing family. What began as a small landscaping business expanded into a successful business. With a background in construction and an eagerness to learn, I began to expand into remodeling and took hands-on projects to build my skills just one year later.

After completing a successful first remodeling project, renovation services were officially added just a few years later. Since then, Shar'on has grown steadily through 20 years of landscaping and 16 years in remodeling. As a fully family-owned business, our team has completed a wide range of projects, from garden maintenance, home additions, bathroom renovations, and electrical work.

Shar'on Garden Landscaping and Renovations remains guided by faith, hard work, and a deep commitment to delivering quality and a reliable service
